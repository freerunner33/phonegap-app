About

La Gran Terraza, located in the Hahn University Center, is USD's own full-service restaurant offering bistro-style ambiance with culinary-certified chefs preparing farm-to-table California cuisine. Dine in the elegant Grand Dining Room, grab a drink and bite to eat in O'Toole's lounge or enjoy your meal al fresco on the terrace, overlooking Mission Bay and Tecolote Canyon. An array of unique culinary events happen throughout the year, including brunch specials, wine dinners, lunch buffets, and cooking classes.
