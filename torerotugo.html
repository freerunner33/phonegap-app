About

Food Trucks are all the rage across the country- and USD is proud to have it's very own. Owned and operated by the University of San Diego Dining Services, Torero Tu Go is the first college self-operated food truck on the West Coast! Recipient of a Silver Loyal E. Horton Award presented by NACUFS for excellence in dining.

The philosophy behind Torero Tu Go’s menu is a reflection of San Diego’s historical culinary culture… Kumeyaay Indian, Portuguese, Mexican, Italian and Asian influences represent a celebration of fresh and seasonal local products from the land, rivers and sea.
