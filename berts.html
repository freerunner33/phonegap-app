About

This spacious bistro, located at the west-end of campus, offers beautiful views of Mission Bay. Enjoy our outdoor barbeque and smoker, hot & cold sandwiches, hand-rolled sushi, soup, salads, and a full espresso and tea bar featuring local, alumni-owned, Ryan Bros. Coffee.
