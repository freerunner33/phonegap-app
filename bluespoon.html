About

The newest addition to campus dining opens July 2015. Blue Spoon offers frozen yogurt, freshly blended juices and smoothies. Conveniently located inside of Frank's Lounge and the new USD Torero Store. Choose your favorite flavor, toppings and boost to create your own refreshing treat!
